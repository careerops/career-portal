Privacy Policy

At Career Opportunities Online, we value transparency. We are a boutique recruitment network connecting global talent with specialized US and International opportunities.

1. How We Use Your Data

The information you submit (Resume, Contact Info, Skill Assessment) is used solely for recruitment purposes. We do not sell your data to third-party marketers or spam lists.

2. Our Partner Network

We operate as a network of hiring partners. By applying, you understand and agree that:

  • Your profile may be reviewed by multiple hiring managers within our partner ecosystem (Logistics, Promo, and Support firms).
  • If the specific role you applied for is filled, we may match your profile with a Partner Agency that has a similar opening.
  • Benefit to You: This significantly increases your chances of placement. If one client role is closed, another partner may review your profile and reach out to you directly via our network.

3. Data Retention & Future Contact

We maintain a secure talent database. Business needs change rapidly; a role that is closed today might reopen next month with a new client. By applying, you agree that we may contact you via email or WhatsApp in the future regarding new opportunities that match your skill set.
